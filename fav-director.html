Bang Woo-ri

Filmmaker & Storyteller
Why she is my favorite

Bang Woo-ri is my favorite director because of how beautifully she crafts her films. Her work on 20th Century Girl is a masterpiece that perfectly captures the bittersweet feelings of youth, first love, and deep friendship.

"Her films don't just tell a story; they make you feel every emotion deeply."

She has a unique way of directing that feels so nostalgic and raw. You don't just watch her movies—you immerse yourself in the lives of the characters, making you feel every joy and heartbreak along with them.

The Beauty of Her Work

From the stunning cinematography to the way she guides her actors to bring out genuine emotions, her artistry is truly special. She knows exactly how to leave a lasting mark on the viewer's heart, turning simple moments into unforgettable memories on screen.
