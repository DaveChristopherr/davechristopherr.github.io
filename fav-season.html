Autumn

The Perfect Escape from the Heat
Why it is my favorite

If I had to choose a perfect season, it would undoubtedly be Autumn. The primary reason is incredibly simple: I cannot stand the unbearable heat. Autumn brings a calm, cooling atmosphere that feels like a quiet transition.

"I hate Summer. Both the season and the character."

There is something deeply comforting about the cold breeze, the falling leaves, and the darker, more muted tones of the environment. It is the perfect weather to stay indoors, write code, play chess, or simply reflect on life without the exhausting humidity.

500 Days of Disappointment

My intense dislike for summer actually goes beyond just the weather. Anyone who has watched the movie 500 Days of Summer knows exactly what I mean.

Just like the season itself, the character Summer was chaotic, draining, and left a trail of heartbreak. It only cemented my stance further. So yes, it is very safe to say—I really hate Summer.
