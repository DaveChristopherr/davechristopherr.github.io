About me

I'm Dave Christopher, also known online as Dave Pham, devxdave. I am a student who spends much of his time reflecting on life and searching for meaning and purpose in a world that often feels uncertain.

I am an atheist. I do not believe in God. My understanding of existence is shaped by observation, experience, and reflection rather than faith. I believe that facing reality honestly, even without comforting answers, is essential to understanding life.

I value quiet thought and intellectual exploration. Programming, philosophy, online communities, games, music, and writing allow me to explore ideas and better understand both myself and the world around me.

I identify as asexual. I have never experienced sexual attraction in person, and I do not seek romantic relationships. I prefer solitude and independence, where reflection and learning can exist without distraction.

My future remains uncertain. I am drawn to programming for its logic and creativity, and to philosophy for the deeper questions it raises about existence, knowledge, and meaning.

If you happen to read this one day, perhaps I am still here, or perhaps I am already gone. Either way, these words capture a moment in my life as someone who continues to question, observe, and search for understanding.
