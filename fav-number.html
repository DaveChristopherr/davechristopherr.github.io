73 & 96

Meaning in the Digits
The Significance of 73

Usually, numbers are just cold, mathematical concepts to me. But 73 and 96 hold a completely different kind of significance.

The number 73 is incredibly special because of Hanni. It is her favorite number, and it is the iconic number she wears on her jersey. I actually have a matching jersey of my own, making this number a quiet, personal tribute to someone I deeply admire.

"Sometimes, a random number becomes unforgettable simply because of the person who said it."
A Deep-Cut Memory: 96

As for 96, the reason behind it is a bit of a deep-cut memory. A long time ago, I was watching a video of Hanni, and out of nowhere, she mentioned "level 96" followed by a phrase in Korean.

I don't even remember exactly which video it was from anymore, but that specific moment just stuck in my head. It is funny how the human mind works—discarding so much random information every day, yet permanently saving a fleeting, passing quote from an idol you look up to.
