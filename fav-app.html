My Daily Soundtrack
Why it is my favorite

Spotify is my absolute favorite app simply because it is incredibly seamless and user-friendly. The interface makes it effortless to find the perfect music or podcast for whatever mood I am in.

"It is safe to say that this is where a lot of my money goes."

I have been a Premium subscriber for about two years now, and it is completely worth it. Aside from the ad-free experience, one of the things I look forward to the most is Spotify Wrapped, which perfectly summarizes my entire year in audio.

A Shared Experience

Music is always better when shared. I actually have a friend who borrows my account, so the two of us get to enjoy the premium experience together. It's nice knowing that the same app provides the soundtrack for both of our daily lives.
