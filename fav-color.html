Why these colors?

My preference for Black and Dark Blue stems from my love for minimalism and the feeling of the "infinite."

Black represents power, elegance, and the void—a blank canvas where everything begins. It is the core of my dark-themed setup and my personal style.

Dark Blue or Navy represents the depth of the ocean and the night sky. It brings a sense of calm and focus, which is essential for someone like me who enjoys deep thinking, chess, and coding.
