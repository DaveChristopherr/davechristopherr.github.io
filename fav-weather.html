Heavy Rain

The Symphony of Solitude
The Ultimate Relaxation

For me, the perfect weather is not a clear, sunny day—it is a heavy downpour. There is something profoundly relaxing about the sound of heavy rain hitting the roof. It naturally drowns out all the chaotic and unnecessary noise of the outside world, replacing it with a calming, consistent rhythm.

"Sometimes, the loudest storms bring the deepest silence."

When it rains heavily, the world is forced to pause. People stay inside, the streets empty out, and everything just slows down. It creates an atmosphere where you don't feel pressured to be anywhere else.

Cold and Quiet

My absolute favorite moments are spent inside my room during these heavy rains. The cold temperature and the isolated quiet create the perfect sanctuary.

It is the best time to just sit in the silence of my room, write code, play chess, or simply get lost in my own thoughts. The heavy rain acts as a barrier that isolates me from the rest of the world, giving me the perfect quiet space I always look for.
